About Us

About Red RPM Production

Red RPM Production is a Florida-based cinematic automotive video production company built for one purpose — to tell the stories that live behind the wheel.

We do not just film cars. We film the people who earned them, built them, and love them. The 25-year-old who just pulled into the driveway in his first Porsche. The builder who spent three years on a single engine swap. The collector who has poured everything into a build that means more than most people will ever understand. Every one of them has a story worth telling — and every one of those stories deserves to be seen the way it actually feels.

Founded by Edwin Adrian Malave, Red RPM Production was built on a lifelong connection to car culture and a belief that the most powerful videos are not the ones that look the best — they are the ones that make you feel something.

Your Car. Your Story.
